Before the war: The nationalist faction consisted of conservative, monarchist, fascist, and traditional forces opposing the republic, aiming to restore traditional order in Spain.

After the war: Nationalists achieved victory, and Francisco Franco established and authoritarian regime. He became the caudillo of Spain, leading a fascist goverment.
